About MIMUC

MIMUC is a student-led conference at the University of Manchester bringing together undergraduates, master's students, researchers, and industry professionals.

The conference focuses on sharing mathematical ideas, exploring interdisciplinary connections, and giving students the opportunity to present topics they are passionate about.

The programme includes student talks alongside invited plenary speakers, as well as networking opportunities and engagement with sponsors.

MIMUC 2026

The 2026 conference welcomed over 200 attendees across two days, with strong participation in student presentations.

The concluding public lecture attracted over 150 attendees from both the university and the wider public.

London Mathematical Society

The London Mathematical Society (LMS), founded in 1865, is the UK’s learned society for mathematics, dedicated to advancing mathematical knowledge, supporting nathematicians and fostering a vibrant community.

Through publications, grants, conferences, and educational initiatives, the LMS supports mathematicians at all stages of their careers.

Student members gain access to leading mathematical journals, networking opportunities, workshops and lectures, as well as resources that support engagement and opportunities to advance mathematical education and research.
